MC SQUARE

Abhishek Bensla aka MC Square was born in the year 1999 in Bhavana, Palwal in Haryana. Basically, he is from Faridabad, Haryana. In an interview, he revealed that his friend named Himanshu Bhatt motivated him to audition for the rap and hip-hop show MTV Hustle.

PARADOX

Paradox in Hustle 2.0 was a contestant. The well-known Indian rapper Paradox was born in Delhi. His appearance in the reality program MTV Hustle 2.0 is what made him most famous. Baadshah served as a judge, and Dino James, Dee MC, King, and EPR served as the competition's Squad Boss.

SHRUSTI

Srushti Tawade, an Indian singer, rapper, writer, and satirist, made a remarkable appearance in an Indian hip-hop talent hunt reality show 'MTV Hustle 2.0' (2022) with her songs such as 'Chill Kinda Guy' and 'Main Nahi Toh Koun.'
